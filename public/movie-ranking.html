The Best Movies According to Seth

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Coach Carter

Coach Carter is a sports drama based on the true story of Ken Carter, a high school basketball coach who prioritizes academics and discipline over athletics, even benching his undefeated team for poor grades. The film highlights themes of responsibility, teamwork, and the importance of education in breaking cycles of poverty and hardship.

Scary Movie

The plot follows a group of teenagers being stalked by a mysterious killer while dealing with their own ridiculous antics and over-the-top stereotypes. Filled with outrageous gags and pop culture references, the film turns classic horror tropes into laugh-out-loud moments.

Blue Streak

Blue Streak is a comedic action film about jewel thief Miles Logan, who hides a stolen diamond in a construction site that later becomes a police station. To retrieve the diamond, Miles pretends to be a detective, inadvertently excelling at solving cases and gaining the respect of his fellow officers. His clever schemes and humorous antics lead to a series of chaotic adventures as he balances his criminal past with his accidental heroism.
